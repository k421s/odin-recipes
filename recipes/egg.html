Egg In A Hole

This is the second recipe on the list. It involves an egg. it's egg in a hole

Ingredients

  • 6-12 large eggs
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoon of bacon grease
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • one slice of bread

Directions

  1. Melt the bacon grease in a non-stick pan over low heat
  2. Cut a 1 1/2 to 2-inch hole from the center of the bread slice.
  3. lay in the hot skillet.
  4. When the side facing down is lightly toasted, about 2 minutes, flip and crack the egg into the hole.
  5. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Continue to cook until the egg is cooked and mostly firm.
  7. Flip again and cook 1 minute more to assure doneness on both sides. Serve immediately.
