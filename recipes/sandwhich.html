Chili & Avacado Turkey Club

You'll not only fall in love with the fresh flavors in this turkey club but also the way it looks.

Ingredients

  • 3 slices of your favorite bread I used buttermilk
  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon salsa verde
  • 1 tablespoon green chiles
  • 1/2 tablespoon sour cream
  • 1/4 jalapeno seeded and finely diced
  • 6 slices smoked turkey breast from the deli
  • 1/2 avocado pitted, sliced, and removed from the skin
  • 1 green lettuce leaf torn into large pieces
